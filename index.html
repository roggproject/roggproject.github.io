Covert Channel TCP/IP's Project Research

 

The primary purpose of our project research is to create a set of two tools, one for “attackers” and one for “defenders”, implementing some of the existing and most interesting theoretical covert channels techniques (and possibly unexplored new ones), as well as means to detect or/and neutralize them. To this end, we divided into two groups: one dedicated to the attack and one dedicated to the defense.
